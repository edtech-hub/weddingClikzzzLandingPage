The Experience

Beautifully Unhurried

A pre-wedding shoot — sometimes called an engagement shoot — is a relaxed yet professional photoshoot of the two of you, prior to the big day. The images become wedding-day décor, save-the-dates, signage and — most of all — keepsakes that document this milestone in your story.

For us, the main focus isn't actually the photographs… it's you. Our priority is to make you feel comfortable and trusting, so your authentic personalities can naturally shine through. Tell us your concerns, your favourite angles, what you'd rather avoid — it all matters.

"Couples who choose a pre-wedding shoot end up with better wedding photography on the day — they already know us, the camera, and themselves on it."
