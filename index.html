Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know

What do you specialise in?+

We specialise in providing all services of photography for all events such as weddings, birthdays, Bat-Mitzvahs and religious ceremonies.

Where are you based and what area do you cover?+

We cover all of the UK and are based in West London (Ruislip). We are also happy to shoot worldwide — travel costs are covered by the client.

Can we visit your studio?+

Yes. We always recommend you come and see the quality of our work and give yourself peace of mind before booking. Call us to arrange an appointment.

Can you cover both sides of a wedding?+

Yes. In fact we work much more efficiently this way as we are all on the same team — there is more cooperation and teamwork.

What is a storybook album?+

A storybook album is a digitally created album containing your chosen photos. Every page is uniquely designed. We have an extensive range that are all carefully crafted.

How do we get our storybook album?+

First you choose your album when booking. After your event, you select your favourite photos. Our designers create stunning pages, emailed to you in draft form for your input before final printing.

Can you take pre-wedding shots?+

Yes. You can add pre-wedding shots to your package. These can be made into a slideshow, enlarged for display at the wedding, or provided on disc. You choose the location — some require permits (costs covered by client).

How long after the event do we get photos?+

We edit your images to their highest quality and provide them on USB or DVD within the first month after your wedding event.

Do you have insurance?+

Yes. We have public liability insurance.
